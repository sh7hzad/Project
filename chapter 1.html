chicken biryani

Chicken Biryani is a fragrant rice dish made with marinated chicken, aromatic spices, and basmati rice, layered and slow-cooked to perfection. Garnished with fried onions, cilantro, and mint, it’s a flavorful and hearty meal, perfect for any occasion.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil or ghee
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon stick, cardamoms, cloves)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water for cooking

METHOD

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté whole spices until fragrant.
  3. Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  5. Add tomatoes, yogurt, and spices; cook until oil separates.
  6. Add chicken pieces and cook until tender.
  7. In a separate pot, boil water, add soaked rice, and cook until 70% done.
  8. Layer the cooked chicken and rice in a pot, garnish with coriander and mint.
  9. Cover and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes (Dum cooking).
  10. Fluff the biryani gently and serve hot with raita.
